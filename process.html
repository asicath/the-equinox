The Process
All PDFs on this site were created by taking scans of first editions of Aleister Crowley's The Equinox, enhancing the black ink and removing the paper texture from the background. The PDFs are composed entirely of images created in this fashion and though this increases their size it has three primary aims: 1. To ensure there can be no typos, 2. To allow one to read the documents in the original type and layout, 3. That all the details of the images are clear

Access to the raw scans is available to those who help support the project with a donation.
